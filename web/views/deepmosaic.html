About DeepMosaic

DeepMosaic is an AI tool for:

  • Mosaic Removal: Intelligently reconstruct faces and objects under mosaic/blur
  • Mosaic Addition: Apply mosaic to specific regions (e.g., face anonymization)
  • Style Transfer: Apply artistic styles to images/videos
  • Traditional Processing: Non-AI mosaic removal using image processing

Mode Details:

  • Clean: AI-based mosaic removal (requires clean_face_HD.pth or clean_youknow_v1.pth)
  • Add: Add mosaic to images (requires add_face.pth)
  • Style: Apply artistic style (requires candy.pth or other style models)
  • Traditional: Non-AI method using blur/downsample - works without models

Tips:

  • For best results, use clear, well-lit images
  • Video processing may take several minutes depending on length
  • Download models from DeepMosaic repository and place in pretrained_models/
  • Traditional mode works without AI models
  • Results are saved in the History section
