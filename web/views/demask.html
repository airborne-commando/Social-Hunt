Forensic AI Demasking

This tool uses a multi-stage AI pipeline to reconstruct facial features. First, visible skin tone, gender cues, and facial hair are sampled from the exposed face area. The gaiter/balaclava region is then pre-filled with the subject's skin colour so the AI sees flesh-coloured pixels rather than a mask — preventing it from generating a replacement covering. Realistic Vision inpainting then fills only the covered region, leaving hair, cap, clothing and background pixel-perfect.

Warning: This feature is experimental. No AI can know what a person's face truly looks like beneath a covering — results are plausible reconstructions, not ground truth. Always verify against primary source material.
