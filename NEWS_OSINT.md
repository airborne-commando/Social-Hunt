# OSINT News Digest

Compiled (UTC): 2026-01-28

Short, curated highlights with sources. Update regularly.

## Highlights

- 2024-03-08: ODNI and CIA released the Intelligence Community OSINT Strategy
  for 2024-2026, outlining coordinated data acquisition, collection
  management, innovation, and workforce development. Source: ODNI.
- 2025-03-26: ODNI released the Intelligence Community Standard for the OSINT
  discipline (ICS-206-01). Source: intelligence.gov.
- 2025-11-19 to 2025-11-20: Council of Europe hosted an AML/CFT-focused OSINT
  conference in Sofia on operational and strategic analysis. Source: Council of
  Europe.
